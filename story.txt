Haunted by his father’s last words, Eirik chose a different path. Instead of revenge, he wandered distant lands in search of a better way to live—one free from endless violence.

He learned to farm, to build, to help those who had suffered from war. Along the way, he met others who had also laid down their swords.

But the world was not kind to pacifists. Raiders razed villages, kings demanded loyalty, and the shadow of war loomed ever closer.

Eirik began to wonder: can peace survive if no one is willing to fight for it?
